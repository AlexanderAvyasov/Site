// HOW IT WORKS

Foot traffic as a
measurable channel.

GeoEarn gives businesses the same accountability they get from digital ads — but for offline visits. Pay per result. Measure everything.

🗺️

Discover Nearby Campaigns

Users open the GeoEarn app — available as a Telegram Mini App — and browse active campaigns near them. Cafés, gyms, retail stores: any physical location can run a campaign with a set cost-per-visit budget.

📲

Walk In & Check In

The user arrives and scans the QR code on-site. GPS location is cross-checked with the business address and the user's visit history to confirm the visit is genuine. No faking it.

⚡

Instant Verified Reward

Once the visit is confirmed, the reward is released to the user's wallet within seconds. The business is charged only for that one verified visit. Every transaction is logged transparently — no black boxes.

// MARKET OPPORTUNITY

Offline commerce is huge.
Performance tools don't exist.

Online advertising has cost-per-click. Offline has nothing equivalent. GeoEarn is building the infrastructure to change that.

$3.8T
US LOCAL RETAIL MARKET

Annual spending at physical stores, restaurants, and service businesses. Every business in this market needs to drive foot traffic — and most have no way to measure it accurately.

$27B
LOYALTY MARKET SIZE

Spent annually on loyalty programs that are siloed, opaque, and non-portable. Points expire. Rewards go unused. GeoEarn replaces this with transparent, instant, liquid rewards.

$2–5
TARGET COST PER VISIT

Businesses on GeoEarn set their own cost-per-visit budget. At $2–5 per confirmed customer walking through the door, this is significantly cheaper than most traditional local advertising.

💡

The gap: A café can run a Google ad campaign and measure every click. But when that same customer walks through the door, there's no equivalent measurement layer. GeoEarn is cost-per-visit infrastructure — filling the last-mile gap between digital reach and physical conversion.

// PRODUCT

Built for businesses,
not crypto users.

GeoEarn works as a straightforward business tool. Blockchain infrastructure runs quietly in the background — merchants and users don't need to understand it.

🎯
Product-first, infrastructure-second. GeoEarn uses blockchain infrastructure selectively — where it adds real value in transparency, instant settlement, and tamper-proof verification. The core product works with or without a crypto wallet. Users who prefer simple cash rewards are supported too.
📍

Verified Visit Engine

A multi-layer verification system combining GPS geofencing, QR scan confirmation, and visit history scoring. Every check-in is independently auditable — businesses are never charged for a visit that didn't happen. No disputes. No click fraud. No bot traffic. Just real customers at the door.

GPS + QR + Visit History Scoring
💎

Instant Rewards

Users receive their reward within seconds of a verified check-in — no waiting period, no minimum balance, no voucher to redeem later. Rewards land directly in the app wallet and can be cashed out or spent.

Instant Settlement
🏪

Business Campaign Manager

Merchants set a cost-per-visit budget, define a geo-zone, and go live in minutes. Pause, adjust, or end campaigns at any time. Pay only when a verified customer walks in — no minimum spend required.

Pay Per Verified Visit
🔧

Transparent Settlement Layer

GeoEarn uses blockchain infrastructure for settlement — not because it's trendy, but because it solves a specific problem: microtransaction settlement at scale with full auditability. At fractions of a cent per transaction, every reward payout is economically viable and every charge is verifiable by the merchant independently. The infrastructure is invisible to the end user.

Transparent · Auditable · Scalable
📊

Analytics Dashboard

Businesses track visit heatmaps, peak hours, campaign ROI, and repeat visitor rates in real time. All data is sourced from verified visits — no estimations, no samples.

// INFRASTRUCTURE

Blockchain where it
actually helps.

GeoEarn uses blockchain infrastructure selectively — for the specific problems it solves better than any alternative: transparent microtransactions, tamper-proof visit logs, and instant reward settlement. Users don't need to understand or interact with crypto to use the product.

🔍

Tamper-proof visit records

Every verified check-in is logged immutably. Businesses can independently verify any charge — there's no central server to dispute with, no billing support ticket to file. The record is just there.

⚡

Economical at micro scale

GeoEarn processes thousands of small reward payouts daily — fractions of a dollar each. The settlement layer handles this at sub-cent cost per transaction, making reward amounts of $0.50–$5 commercially viable even for small businesses.

🙈

Invisible to end users

Users earn rewards and businesses run campaigns without ever needing a crypto wallet or understanding how the settlement layer works. The product is designed for mainstream adoption, not crypto users.

Not crypto-dependent. GeoEarn's core product — visit verification, campaign management, and reward distribution — is independent of any specific blockchain. The infrastructure is a component, not the product.
