How It Works

Foot traffic as a measurable channel.

GeoEarn gives businesses the same accountability they get from digital ads — but for offline visits. Pay per result. Measure everything.

1
Discover Nearby Campaigns
Users open the GeoEarn Telegram Mini App and browse active campaigns nearby. Cafés, gyms, retail stores — any physical location can run a campaign with a set cost-per-visit budget.
2
Walk In & Check In
The user arrives and scans the QR code on-site. GPS location is cross-checked with the business address and the user's visit history to confirm the visit is genuine.
3
Instant Verified Reward
Once verified, GEO coins land in the user's wallet within seconds. The business is charged only for that one confirmed visit — every transaction logged transparently.

Product

Built for businesses,
not crypto users.

GeoEarn works as a straightforward business tool. Blockchain infrastructure runs quietly in the background — merchants and users don't need to understand it.

Verified Visit Engine
A multi-layer verification system combining GPS geofencing, QR scan confirmation, and visit history scoring. Every check-in is independently auditable — businesses are never charged for a visit that didn't happen.
GPS + QR + Visit History Scoring
Instant Rewards
Users receive GEO within seconds of a verified check-in — no waiting period, no minimum balance, no voucher to redeem later.
Instant Settlement
Campaign Manager
Set a cost-per-visit budget, define a geo-zone, and go live in minutes. Pause, adjust, or end campaigns at any time. Pay only when a verified customer walks in.
Pay Per Verified Visit
Transparent Settlement Layer
GeoEarn uses blockchain infrastructure for settlement — not because it's trendy, but because it solves a specific problem: microtransaction settlement at scale with full auditability. At fractions of a cent per transaction, every reward payout is economically viable and every charge is verifiable by the merchant independently.
Transparent · Auditable · Scalable
Analytics Dashboard
Businesses track visit heatmaps, peak hours, campaign ROI, and repeat visitor rates in real time. All sourced from verified visits.

Market Opportunity

Offline commerce is huge.
Performance tools don't exist.

Online advertising has cost-per-click. Offline has nothing equivalent. GeoEarn is building the infrastructure to change that.

$3.8T
US Local Retail Market
Annual spending at physical stores, restaurants, and service businesses. Every business in this market needs to drive foot traffic — and most have no way to measure it accurately.
$27B
Loyalty Market Size
Spent annually on loyalty programs that are siloed, opaque, and non-portable. Points expire. Rewards go unused. GeoEarn replaces this with transparent, instant, liquid rewards.
$2–5
Target Cost Per Visit
Businesses on GeoEarn set their own cost-per-visit budget. At $2–5 per confirmed customer walking through the door, this is significantly cheaper than most traditional local advertising.

The gap: A café can run a Google ad campaign and measure every click. But when that same customer walks through the door, there's no equivalent measurement layer. GeoEarn is cost-per-visit infrastructure — filling the last-mile gap between digital reach and physical conversion.

Infrastructure

Blockchain where it actually helps.

GeoEarn uses blockchain infrastructure selectively — for transparent microtransactions, tamper-proof visit logs, and instant reward settlement. Users don't need to interact with crypto.

Tamper-proof visit records
Every verified check-in is logged immutably. Businesses can independently verify any charge — there's no central server to dispute with, no billing support ticket to file.
Economical at micro scale
Thousands of small reward payouts daily at sub-cent cost per transaction, making reward amounts of $0.50–$5 commercially viable even for small businesses.
Invisible to end users
Users earn rewards and businesses run campaigns without ever needing a crypto wallet. The product is designed for mainstream adoption, not crypto users.
