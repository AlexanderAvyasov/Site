// WHY SOLANA

The only chain
that scales offline.

GeoEarn's reward model only works if transaction costs are negligible. On Ethereum, fees would exceed the reward value itself. Solana makes microtransaction-based commerce viable at scale.

⚡
Instant reward distribution
Users expect their reward the moment they scan. Solana's 400ms block time means settlement feels instantaneous — no "pending" state, no friction at the check-in moment.
💸
Microtransaction economics that work
At 1,000 daily visits, GeoEarn processes 1,000 reward transactions per day. At $0.00025 each, the total settlement cost is $0.25/day. Any other chain makes this model impossible.
🔍
Transparent visit verification
Every check-in is logged on-chain. Businesses can verify the source of every charge independently — no black boxes, no trust required, no billing disputes.
